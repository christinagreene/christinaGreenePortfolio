Hi, I'm Christina!

A detail-oriented, design-focused Front-End Developer passionate about providing accessible and pixel-perfect user experiences.

I am a recent graduate of Juno College of Technology’s Bootcamp (formerly HackerYou), where I received a Web Development Immersive Certificate. I love designing and creating all styles of web software, from basic single page sites to innovative and complex applications.

Creativity has always been present in my life and I am passionate about translating my artistic abilities to the web. I enjoy building clean-coded websites and web applications from scratch and strive to ensure they are seamless and engaging experiences that leave a positive impact on the user. My meticulous and analytical thought process allows me to face problems head on while working towards efficient solutions.

I strongly believe that personal and professional development stems from constant learning and my love for education has encouraged me to continue finding new and improved ways to better myself and my code. I am always looking for opportunities to work with people who are willing to collaborate and share their knowledge in an effort to learn from them.

When I’m not on my computer, you can find me baking (delicious) gluten-free treats, burning up a sweat at hot yoga or playing softball.
